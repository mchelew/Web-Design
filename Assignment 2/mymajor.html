My Academic History

My School's

I graduated high school from Ursuline Academy in Kirkwood, Missouri. I chose to attend Saint Louis University for my first two years of college. After realizing that it isn't the best fit for me I decided to transfer to The Ohio State University and major in Fashion Design. After a year and a half I missed my friends and family too much and moved back to Saint Louis and that is when I decided to attend Lindenwood University.

My Major

Since I bounced around from school to school it was difficult to stick with one major as well. When I got to Lindenwood I decided to major in Graphic Design.
